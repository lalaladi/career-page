Frequently Asked Questions

    What?
  • Is this internship paid or unpaid?

    Unfortunately, at the moment, the internship program at Dialogika remains unpaid, as we are still a new and developing company and lack the financial stability to provide monetary compensation. We can only hope for better prospects in the future for our interns.

  • Is there any compensation provided by the company, such as transportation and lunch?

    Unfortunately, we are unable to provide perks such as lunch and transportation at this time. Occasionally, Dialogika organizes group meals during certain events, but these are still quite rare. If you find this unsatisfactory, we kindly ask that you refrain from applying to this program.

  • What if I don't complete the internship program?

    If you are unable to complete this internship program, it is regrettable that you will not receive an eCertificate from us, and it is also unfortunate that if any companies ask about you in the future, we will not be able to speak much about you. Make sure your heart is ready if you want to join.

    • Why?
  • Why should I join this program?

    While we appreciate your interest in joining our internship program, we do not recommend it if you are solely looking for a source of income. Our program aims to equip you with the mental and professional skills necessary to succeed in the workforce. We also have a unique working environment where we work and have fun at the same time, though not everyone may find it suitable. However, many of our interns have gone on to become key members of our team at Dialogika.

    • How?
  • How can I apply for this internship?

    To apply for this internship, simply fill out the "Registration Form" located at the bottom of this page and complete all required fields. Please note that we do not require you to send us your CV or resume.

    We kindly request that you do not make your Instagram and TikTok accounts private, as our selection process begins there. Also, please ensure that your face is clearly visible so we can recognize you. Thank you for your understanding.

Mengertilah Bahwa Internship Adalah Latihan Kerja

Mengerti bahwa internship adalah latihan kerja, bukan latihan organisasi kampus, yang berarti kamu memahami bahwa berkomitmen internship setara dengan komitmen kerja

Tiga Bulan Internship

Mampu melakukan internship selama 3 bulan di Dialogika. Apabila belum bisa atau ada permintaan khusus dari kampus diharapkan melapor terlebih dahulu

Mendapatkan Izin Orang Tua

Pastikan sebelum join kamu sudah mendapatkan izin dari orang tua, kami tidak ingin internshipmu terhenti karena tidak dapat izin dari orang tua

Tidak Bucin

Aturan ini serius! Jika kamu punya pacar dan masih harus dapat izin dari pacarmu untuk ikutan internship atau kegiatan apapun. Saran kami jangan join ke Dialogika.

Care Dengan Penampilan

Menjaga dan terus memperbaiki penampilan adalah bagian dari core yang ada di Dialogika, karena product Dialogika adalah public speaking. Dan berlatih menjaga penampilan adalah bagian dari representasi yang ditampilkan di Dialogika
